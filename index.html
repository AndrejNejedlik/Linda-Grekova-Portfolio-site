aBOUT ME

Linda is a outstandingly talented artist with big imagination since she was a child. The beauty of nature inspires her as well as different life situations with a bit of a fantasy. She won various art competitions when she was just 5 years old. In a few years time she had her first art exhibition and now, as 18 years old, her paintings are hanging on walls in Sydney, Taipei, Kuala Lumpur, Oregon and many others.
